Historic Online Quiz Rules

  1. Quiz Overview: The historic online quiz consists of 10 multiple-choice questions related to historical events, figures, and landmarks.
  2. Single Submission: Each user is allowed to submit the quiz only once. Multiple submissions will not be accepted, and only the first attempt will be considered for evaluation.
  3. Question Navigation: Users can only proceed to the next question after submitting an answer for the current question.
  4. Answer Submission: For each question, users must choose one correct answer from the provided options and then click the "Submit Answer" button.
  5. Answer Confirmation: Once the user submits an answer, they will receive immediate feedback on their response, indicating whether the answer was correct or incorrect.
  6. Next Question: After submitting an answer and receiving feedback, users can click the "Next Question" button to proceed to the next question.
  7. Final Submission: After answering all 10 questions, the quiz will be automatically submitted for evaluation.
  8. Evaluation and Results: The quiz will be automatically scored, and users will be presented with their final score along with the correct answers for each question.
  9. Feedback: Users will receive immediate feedback on their performance, highlighting the questions they answered correctly and those they answered incorrectly.
  10. Honesty and Fair Play: Users are expected to answer the questions honestly and without seeking external assistance. Any attempt to cheat or use unauthorized resources is strictly prohibited and may result in disqualification.
  11. Results Sharing: Users may have the option to share their quiz results on social media or with friends, celebrating their achievements or challenging others to try the quiz.
  12. Fun and Educational: The historic online quiz aims to be both fun and educational, encouraging users to test their knowledge of historical events and learn new facts along the way.
